John Wick 1

Considering its origins as a pretty straightforward revenge story, the John Wick action movie franchise could easily have continued churning out boilerplate sequels. Instead, it has swiftly created a fictional world steeped in arcane lore which becomes more complex with each new chapter. The films take place within an unseen demimonde, populated by assassins who adhere to all kinds of ancient traditions, and abide by a certain code. In that way, the Keanu Reeves-fronted series isn't that far removed from the Star Wars movies, which boast a similarly mysterious order of warriors in the form of Jedi Knights. So, in a move which absolutely nobody asked for but yielded some pretty amazing results, special effects YouTube channel ImmersionVFX put a lightsaber in Keanu Reeves' hand. Using footage from the most recent installment, John Wick Chapter 3 - Parabellum, editors digitally inserted lightsabers into a series of sword fights from the movie, turning what was already a hi-octane thrill ride into a neon-infused glimpse at just how well-suited the preternaturally chill Keanu would be to playing a Jedi if the inclination ever arose.

Me Before You

Me Before You is a 2016 romantic drama film directed by Thea Sharrock in her directorial debut and adapted by English author Jojo Moyes from her 2012 novel of the same name. The film stars Emilia Clarke, Sam Claflin, Jenna Coleman, Charles Dance, Matthew Lewis, Janet McTeer, Vanessa Kirby, Joanna Lumley, and Steve Peacocke. Set in the United Kingdom, the film is shot in various historic locations around the country, including Pembroke Castle in Wales, and Chenies Manor House in Buckinghamshire, England. Released on June 3, 2016, in North America, the film received mixed reviews and grossed $208 million worldwide
